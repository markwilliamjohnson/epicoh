This is a simple negative feedback loop: the controller compares the reference to the delayed, noisy perception, generating an output that acts on the environment. The graph shows how well the world variable (and thus perception) is kept near the reference while a sinusoidal disturbance pushes it around. Increase gain or delay to see overshoot and oscillations; reduce gain or disturbance for smooth control.
