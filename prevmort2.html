Interactive CHD Dynamics Simulator — with AMI as a Subset of CHD

This version separates AMI (acute MI) from other CHD and distinguishes true vs observed prevalence using diagnostic coverage (including a female under‑diagnosis gap). It clarifies numerators/denominators and lets you focus on AMI‑only or all‑CHD views.

Scale directions
  • Lifestyle factor: 0.5 = healthier (lower risk) → 1.0 baseline → 1.5 unhealthier (higher risk).
  • Primary prevention: 0% = none → higher % = stronger incidence reduction (risk factor control, statins, diabetes management).
  • Treatment quality (AMI / Other CHD): higher % reduces mortality among existing cases (post‑MI care vs HF/angina care).
  • Diagnostic coverage: fraction of true cases that are diagnosed/recorded; female gap reduces coverage for women (assume 50/50 split).

Interpretation & Specificity

  • Two stocks: PAMI (AMI survivors) and POTH (other CHD). Total true CHD prevalence is P = PAMI + POTH.
  • Flows: new AMI cases IAMI·(1−P), new other‑CHD cases IOTH·(1−P), deaths MAMI·PAMI and MOTH·POTH.
  • Observed prevalence = (c − g/2) · P where c = diagnostic coverage and g = female coverage gap (assuming 50/50 sex split and equal prevalence).
  • Scope toggles what counts in numerators/denominators for prevalence, incidence, and mortality displays: AMI‑only vs all‑CHD, true vs observed.
Model: IAMI = IAMI,base × lifestyle × (1 − prevention), IOTH = IOTH,base × lifestyle × (1 − prevention), MAMI = MAMI,base × (1 − treatAMI), MOTH = MOTH,base × (1 − treatOTH), Pt+1 = Pt + IAMI(1−Pt) + IOTH(1−Pt) − MAMIPAMI − MOTHPOTH.
