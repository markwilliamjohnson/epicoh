Weird World Rules

In this world, the normal laws of logic and mathematics don't apply. Here are some of the bizarre rules:

  • Numbers change their values based on your mood
  • Addition sometimes subtracts, and multiplication sometimes divides
  • The number 7 is considered unlucky and reverses operations
  • Prime numbers have special properties that alter reality
  • Zero is both everything and nothing at the same time
  • Negative numbers create portals to other dimensions
